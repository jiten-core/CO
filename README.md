# CO

A comprehensive scientific report and interactive website on **Carbon Monoxide (CO)** — covering its properties, sources, health effects, detection, prevention, and treatment.

## Contents

- **Scientific Report** (`message.txt`) — A detailed, evidence-based report (~4,000–6,000+ words) covering:
  - Physical & chemical properties of CO
  - Sources of carbon monoxide exposure
  - Mechanisms of CO toxicity
  - Health effects & symptoms by exposure level
  - Diagnosis and clinical management
  - Prevention strategies & safety measures
  - Global statistics & public health impact

## Purpose

This project aims to raise awareness about the dangers of carbon monoxide — a colorless, odorless, and tasteless gas that is one of the leading causes of accidental poisoning worldwide.
